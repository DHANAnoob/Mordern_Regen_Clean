    The Treatment

    We offer one of the most effective treatment, which is a combination of Fluoroscopy, Arthrogram, and AARP injection

    Fluoroscopy

    Real-time X-ray imaging for accurate diagnosis of joint pain and inflammation.

    Arthrogram

    Detailed view of joint structures with contrast dye to identify tears, loose bodies, and inflammation.

    AARP injection

    Powerful healing agent with concentrated platelets, lubricant to reduce friction and pain, and medication to reduce inflammation.


    When used in combination, it can be a highly effective treatment for your osteoarthritis pain. This treatment combination can help to reduce pain, improve function, and slow the progression of the condition.
